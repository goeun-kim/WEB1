Educational Testing Service (ETS) is a nonprofit institution with the mission to advance quality and equity in education by providing fair and valid assessments.

In serving individuals, educational institutions, and government agencies around the world, we customize solutions to meet the need for teacher professional development products and services, classroom and end-of-course assessments, and research-based teaching and learning tools.

Founded in 1947, ETS develops, administers, and scores more than 24 million tests annually in more than 180 countries, at over 9,000 locations around the world.
